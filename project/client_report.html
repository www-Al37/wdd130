General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part was definitely fulfilling my role as a lead developer because I worked on that the most and ran into some tricky challenges along the way, but I was able to push through them. The part I enjoyed the most was definitely coming up with the meme and philosophy idea as the client because it was just so good to see that concept come together.
